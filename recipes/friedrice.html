Fried RICE

Fried rice is one very popular dish. Like the name suggest, it is just regular rice fried instead of regular boiling or steaming. Lets take a look at the ways to make it and what you need in the making

Ingredients

  • Rice obviously
  • onion, vegetables
  • salt, and pepper
  • fish or chicken or protein of your choice

Steps to cooking

  1. Wash and prepare the rice
  2. boil or fried the meat or fish and set aside
  3. Prepare your vegetable, onion, carrots, etc
  4. Place, the rice in hot pan and add, let to fry while turning it.
  5. Add, your spieces to rice, stir, add, add, and add the fish or meat, cove rand let to cook
  6. Cook for about 20 minutes and thats it, you've got yourself a yummy decicious dish

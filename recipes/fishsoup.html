Fish Soup

Fish soup like the main simply means a soup made of different kinds of fishes or maybe just one fish but with improved flavour and added ingredients

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish
  • spices
  • salt and pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Clean and prepare your fish properly
  2. Place the fish in a pot, add enough water and let to boil
  3. When it starts to boil, add salt, pepper, and other ingredients
